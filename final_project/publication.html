Habitats and care for baboons and monkeys

We brought Tilin to a sanctuary in the UK in 2010, building a habitat and providing food and care, but he needed company.

ADI Wildlife Sanctuary

The sanctuary was born out of tragedy. Our 33 lions from the Peru and Colombia rescue had been taken to Emoya Big Cat Sanctuary (now Waterburg Wildlife Rescue) in 2016, where we had built facilities and funded their care.

Wildlife & environment

ADI supported increased protection under the Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species for giraffe, African elephants, big cats, and opposed weakening protections for rhinos and elephants.

Help end bullfighting in Colombia!

ADI and fellow animal protection groups in Colombia, under the coalition Colombia Sin Toreo, are campaigning to ban bullfighting nationwide.

Animals in Movies & Video

Video released in March 2011 by ADI showing elephants owned by US performing animal supplier Have Trunk Will Trunk being beaten and electric-shocked during training shocked the world and led to a lawsuit and an ongoing campaign by ADI to end elephant rides.

Pull penguins from Ski and Snowboard Show

In the wild, penguins spend most of their lives in the water and the complexity of these animals does not make them easy to look after.

ADI slates companies using performing animals

It is not only physical punishment or withdrawal of food or water that encourages animals to perform. The trainer is often the sole source of contact, approval and affection as well and this power can be used to make the animal comply.

Potomac Nationals baseball team to host Cowboy Monkey Derby

ADI has contacted the Potomac Nationals to xpress our dismay and ask that they immediately cancel the Cowboy Monkey Rodeo and adopt a “no animals” policy for future events.

Support the ORCA Act

The Orca Responsibility and Care Advancement Act of 2015 (HR4019) seeks to prohibit the import, export, and taking of orcas and orca products, and to prohibit breeding for exhibition.
