TEAPSPA reintroduced!

The Traveling Exotic Animal & Public Safety Protection Act (TEAPSPA), to ban wild and exotic traveling animal acts in the US, was reintroduced to the 117th Congress on November 17. Watch a replay of the launch event and urge your legislators to support bills HR5999 and S3220.

New Jersey bans cosmetics tested on animals

New Jersey has banned the sale of cosmetic products tested on animals, effective March 1, 2022. Governor Phil Murphy signed bills S1726/A795 into law, which applies to products tested on animals after January 1, 2020.

Who we are, what we do

We create public awareness of animal suffering and combine this with scientific and economic research. We provide technical briefings to legislators and governments. ADI rescues animals all over the world and educates the public on animals and environmental issues.

Animal Rescues
Having secured and helped enforce an animal circus ban in Bolivia, ADI returned to Latin America to assist the authorities in Peru and Colombia. Over 100 animals were rescued during Operation Spirit of Freedom.
